Research Area

I study network security analysis and planning and work on problems at the intersection of security, optimization, and machine learning. I have previously worked on systems security projects and developed modified Linux kernels for Desktop and Android with enhanced security. The main projects I'm working on are:

  • the use of moving target defense for defending cloud-based networks against denial of service attacks,
  • studying application-layer behavior in IoT networks to prevent the spread of deceitful data generated by compromised device, and
  • designing security protocols for mobile ad hoc networks.
My research objective is to develop formal models that can describe security strategies and attack possibilities. Thus, my work is at the border of theory of security and applied security mechanisms.
